The Wardrobe Constitution

Fourteen articles, each one collapsible. Draw a random tenet for the day, or jump straight to an article from the index.

My wardrobe is a collection of garments that support a creative, practical, and intentional life. It values craftsmanship over trends, longevity over novelty, and personal expression over fashion cycles.

Every piece should have a purpose, age gracefully, and contribute to a wardrobe that feels unmistakably mine.

I do not build a wardrobe to impress others. I build one to support a life of curiosity, craftsmanship, creativity, and care. My clothing is meant to be worn, repaired, remembered, and enjoyed. Every piece should contribute to a collection that feels authentic, functional, and enduring.

Own less by choosing better. Wear often. Repair proudly. Buy with intention.
